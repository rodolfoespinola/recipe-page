Speculoos or Spekulatius Cookies

Imagine a cookie that's both crispy and rich, with the warm, floral aroma of cinnamon and the deeply caramelized sweetness of toasted brown sugar. That's Speculoos, and you're about to make them yourself!

Ingredients

  • 250 g all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tablespoon baking powder
  • 140 g refined sugar
  • 125 g cold unsalted butter
  • 50 g almond flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom

Instructions

  1. Combine the ingredients until a smooth dough forms, avoiding over-handling.
  2. Once smooth, refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  3. Flour a work surface and roll out the dough, cutting shapes with cookie cutters, keeping the remaining dough refrigerated.
  4. Bake in a preheated oven at 180 degrees Celsius (350 degrees Fahrenheit).
  5. Let cool on a wire rack and sprinkle with cinnamon sugar.

Yields: Approximately 600g of cookies.

Topping: Vanilla sugar and cinnamon for sprinkling after baking.
